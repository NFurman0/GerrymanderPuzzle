Gerrymandering Puzzle:

Your Purple party has a minority in this election, but luckily you've been given the power to draw the county map however you see fit!

Each county must consist of the same number of districts, and cannot contain a tie.

All districts in a county must connect to each other orthogonally.

Use the submit button to submit a county, the reset button to reset the board, and ctrl+z or ctrl+y to undo and redo your last submissions.

Some puzzles may be impossible to solve.
